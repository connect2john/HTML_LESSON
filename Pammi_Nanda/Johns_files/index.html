ABOUT US


Pammi Nanda Foundation (PNF) was founded in 2011 as a charitable organization. We have clearance of 12A tax free status. We are regularly audited by government and our corporate auditors and believe in complete honesty and transparency.

Pammi Nanda Foundation has existed on the basic principle of equality for all and especially to those who do not possess it. An innumerable number of the underprivileged population aspires to be the beneficiary of socio-economic and medical schemes.

OUR MISSON


Our mission has sought to provide for the underprivileged with education and to contribute to society positively and advance human achievement. We believe that education is the paramount pre-requisite of a comfortable life and everyone should have a stake in being informed, as knowledge is power, which eventually leads to progress.

We strengthen our foundation everyday by providing to the needy and to make thousands of people happy by providing education. Over the past 08 years our mission and vision have guided us through transformations in the foundation, the communities we serve, and the society at large.

To see a smile on somebody’s face who didn’t have a reason to do so is the greatest part about our initiative. Not only does it leave our hearts content but also benefits the society at large to the path of progress and development. We believe that there exists an inherent opportunity for all people, but around us, too many people are excluded from the socio-economic, medical and social institutions that shape their lives.

FREE EYE CAMPS
The annual event of holding free eye camps in association with Lions Club, Dehradun was held twice during the last year. We are pleased to inform that more than 300 people were provided with free eye check up and 40 were provided with corrective surgery for their eye aliments at Mahant Indresh Hospital. The patients visiting the camps were also provided with spectacles at subsided rates and free medication. The response to the eye camps organised by PNF has been growing over the years and has now become as an awaited event with the local population in the villages surrounding Dehradun.


COMPUTER TRAINING

Rewari Centre
We shifted our centre from Bhondsi (Haryana) to Rewari (Haryana) on completion of our mission at Bhondsi. We are now running this center at village Rampura, District Rewari. This is being run in association with anon-for profit organization Chetenalaya, who are involved in upliftment of slums at various areas in Haryana and Delhi. We are pleased to inform that an enthusiastic response has been received from the children and the centre has been fully utilized. We have had 80 students who have successfully completed the course at the centre in the last year.


Dehradun Centre
The flagship computer centre at Defence Colony, Dehradun has been growing at a phenomenal pace with the courses being oversubscribed and children travelling from across Dehradun to take these courses. The courses have been running to their maximum capacity for the 8th year in a row and the next few courses already have children enrolling for them.

Brig SP Nanda, VSM, Managing Trustee of the foundation work has been acclaimed by one and all and he was recently featured in the online magazine “My Life Page ” (http://lifepage.app.link/2018022-001)for his selfless contribution to the society. At the Dehradun center we have had 90 students successfully finishing their course in the last year and due to the popularity of the courses being conducted, some senior citizens have also attended the courses


LIFE SKILLS TRAINING
Dehradun centre has in addition to imparting computer skills has taken on the onus of covering some aspects of life skills as part of the course. The centre has seen keen interest being shown by the students towards the guest lectures on the life skill training that is being imparted as part of the course on Fridays like: - First Aid, Current Issues, Moral Values, Environmental Issues, and Basic Healthcare.
